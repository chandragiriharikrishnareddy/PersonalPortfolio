Welcome to Our Bvsr College Landing Page

The BVSR Engineering College was started in 2008 and inaugurated by Dr. Y.S. Rajasekhar Reddy Ex.C.M, A.P. BVSREC located in CHIMAKURTHY, Prakasam Dt. of Andhra Pradesh. It is located on a sprawling 13 acres of land surrounded by green fields. The College is affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
